# 26.1 Explain Decisions

Phase: Interview / VP Level
Topic: Communication
Status: Ready for expansion

## Fast Goal
State what you chose, why you chose it, and what tradeoff you accepted.

## Brain Hook
Good technical communication is clear decision framing, not word volume.

## Session Flow
- frame one decision
- state options considered
- state tradeoff
- summarize recommendation
